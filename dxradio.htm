    • WSPRNet

      WSPR (Weak Signal Packet Reporting)

    • PSK Reporter

      An excellent tool for spotting FT-8, digital modes and CW.

    • WorldsSTV.com

      Live images of what is being seen by SSTV receivers around the world.

  • Digital Software

    • FLDGI

      Multi-purpose software for both receiving and transmitting many common digital modes.

    • WSJT-X

      Software used for WSPR, FT-8 and many other weak signal modes.

    • MMSTV

      Software for Slow Scan TV receiving/transmitting.

    • FT8Call

      A derivative of the open-source FT-8 mode, designed for rag chew. Very experimental.
