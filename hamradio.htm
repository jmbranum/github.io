60 meter band

Back

  • USA: General, Advanced, Extra Classes
    • USB Phone, CW, RTTY & Digital: Channelized on 5330.5, 5346.5,
      5357, 5357.1 and 5403.5 kHz.
      Special rules apply to 60 meters.

  • Canada: Simplified band plan (follow link for details from RAC)
    • Basic Plus Honors & Advanced Classes
      (follow link for details from RAC):
      • CW, Phone, Digital: Channelized on 5330.5, 5346.5, 5357, 5357.1, 5403.5 kHz.
      • CW, Phone, Digital (domestic only): 5329 kHz.
        Special rules apply for 60 meter band, follow link for
        details from RAC.

    • ITU Regions

      • Varies widely by country and region. See ITU for details and special rules.
