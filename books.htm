Introduction

This library is a curated collection of books (mostly from Project Gutenberg and Project Gutenberg Australia, but also from some other sources) curated and shared for access via low bandwidth internet. The works in most cases are either in the public domain or released under a creative commons license. We may in the future also include additional resources under fair use provisions.
