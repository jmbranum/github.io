The Soñador Network is an experiment on creating content for low bandwidth situations. Most of the pages in this network are text-only or at least very small file sizes in the rare cases when graphics are used. This page is hosted from different locations (including at times a repurposed ancient chromebook) and you are alway welcome to mirror the site on your network by downloading one of the periodic zip file archives.
